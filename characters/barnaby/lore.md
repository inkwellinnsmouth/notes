# Barnaby "Barnacle" Bill

## Backstory
Barnaby is a young, optimistic deckhand working for the Innsmouth Shipping Co. He genuinely believes he's just a regular sailor delivering completely normal crates of "fresh fish" (which often squirm and glow) to the townsfolk. He is entirely oblivious to the creeping cosmic horror around him, interpreting horrifying eldritch phenomena as quirky weather or just "the salty sea air."

## The Anchor Tattoo
Barnaby got his anchor tattoo in a shady parlor down by the Innsmouth docks. He thinks it's a remarkably realistic tattoo; he doesn't realize the ink was mixed with the ichor of a minor star-spawn. The eye in the tattoo watches things behind Barnaby's back and occasionally shifts the anchor's shape to point toward areas of high cosmic activity.

## The Glowing Kelp
Instead of spinach or a power-up potion, Barnaby occasionally finds "lucky seaweed" washed up on his boat. Eating it gives him a massive burst of frenzied energy, during which his arm temporarily morphs into a massive, muscular squid tentacle, allowing him to perform superhuman feats of lifting or punching before he snaps back to normal, slightly dazed.
