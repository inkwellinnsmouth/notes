# Storyboard: Barker's Morning Commute
**Total Duration: 1 minute (Six 10-second blocks)**

**Block 1 (0:00 - 0:10)**
- **Action/Narrative:** Barker wakes up, hits his screaming alarm clock with a mallet, and does a bouncy, squash-and-stretch morning routine.
- **Start Frame Prompt:** Barker Blot fast asleep in a sagging, crooked 1920s bed, a jagged screaming mouth replacing an alarm clock nearby. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal sketch, high contrast, heavy film grain. Reference: `characters/barker_blot/images/barker_blot_front.png`
- **End Frame Prompt:** Barker Blot standing cheerfully next to a cracked mirror holding an oversized mallet, his body stretching tall in a classic squash-and-stretch pose. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal sketch, pie-cut eyes, heavy film grain.

**Block 2 (0:10 - 0:20)**
- **Action/Narrative:** Barker steps out of his dilapidated house, trips, and smoothly Charleston-dances entirely down the stairs into the driver's seat of his Model-T jalopy.
- **Start Frame Prompt:** Barker Blot opening the crooked front door of a leaning boarding house, tripping forward mid-air with legs flailing. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal sketch, wide cinematic 16:9 framing, heavy film grain.
- **End Frame Prompt:** Barker Blot seated triumphantly and happily behind the steering wheel of a bouncing, sputtering vintage Model-T jalopy outside his house. 1920s rubber-hose animation, pie-cut eyes, monotone charcoal sketch, heavy film grain.

**Block 3 (0:20 - 0:30)**
- **Action/Narrative:** The jalopy bounces happily down the dirt road until a thick coastal fog rolls in, muting his ragtime tune into an echoing drone.
- **Start Frame Prompt:** A bouncy Model-T jalopy with Barker Blot driving cheerfully down a dusty path. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal sketch, high contrast, heavy film grain.
- **End Frame Prompt:** The jalopy driving down "The Path Into Innsmouth" (ref: `locations/path_into_innsmouth/prompt.md`), completely enveloped and darkened by extremely thick, pervasive coastal fog. Monotone charcoal sketch, heavy film grain, spooky atmospheric lighting.

**Block 4 (0:30 - 0:40)**
- **Action/Narrative:** A small pothole suddenly splits open into an abyssal chasm lined with impossible teeth. The jalopy screech-halts, terror-stricken.
- **Start Frame Prompt:** The bouncy Model-T approaching a small, seemingly mundane pothole in the thick fog. Monotone charcoal sketch, cinematic 16:9 framing, heavy film grain.
- **End Frame Prompt:** The jalopy screech-halted at the very edge of a massive, impossibly deep chasm lined with jagged teeth, the jalopy's physical headlights bulging wide in sheer terror like pie-cut eyes. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal wash, heavy fog.

**Block 5 (0:40 - 0:50)**
- **Action/Narrative:** Barker faces true cosmic horror, completely losing form and melting into his signature puddle of black ink on the floorboards.
- **Start Frame Prompt:** Close-up on Barker Blot sitting in the jalopy, his pie-cut eyes impossibly wide in sheer existential terror, face stretching backwards. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal sketch, heavy film grain.
- **End Frame Prompt:** A small puddle of completely unraveled black ink pooling on the jalopy's wooden floorboards; Barker's flat cap suspended mid-air above the puddle, with two frantic pie-cut eyes darting around inside the ink. Monotone charcoal sketch, high contrast, heavy film grain.

**Block 6 (0:50 - 1:00)**
- **Action/Narrative:** A massive shadowy tentacle sweeps out, casually drops the jalopy on the other side. Barker zips back into shape and continues his bouncy drive into town.
- **Start Frame Prompt:** Wide shot of a colossal, shadowy Cthulhu tentacle sweeping silently out of the heavy coastal fog, wrapping around the tiny jalopy at the edge of the chasm. Monotone charcoal sketch, pervasive fog, heavy film grain.
- **End Frame Prompt:** Barker Blot cleanly zipped back into his solid human form inside the driving jalopy, cheerfully tipping his flat cap towards the dense fog, as the dark, twisted silhouette of Innsmouth looms ahead. 1920s rubber-hose animation, monotone charcoal sketch, heavy film grain.
