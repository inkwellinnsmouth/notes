# Episode: A Delivery for Dagon

**Working Title:** "A Delivery for Dagon"
**Logline:** Plucky delivery boy Barker Blot tries to drop off a mysterious package at a ramshackle roadside bait shop outside Innsmouth, only to discover the locals – and the souvenirs – are far more Eldritch than they appear.

## The Beat Sheet & LTX Prompts

This document breaks down the story beat sheet into specific shots and provides the exact text prompts for LTX Desktop to generate the 6-20 second video clips.

### Shot 1: The Arrival
**Shot Type:** Wide Shot
**Act 1: The Setup (The 1920s Trope)**
**Action:** A bouncy, dilapidated delivery jalopy sputters down a winding dirt road, stopping in front of a top-heavy, warped shack marked "Fresh Bait". Heavy fog rolls in.
**Audio Note:** (Optional) Sputtering engine, peppy ragtime music fades in over howling wind.

```text
A dilapidated 1920s delivery truck bouncing elastically as it sputters to a halt on a winding dirt road. A warped, top-heavy wooden shack in the background. Heavy thick coastal fog rolling in dynamically. Camera slowly pushes in. A vintage 1920s rubber-hose style cartoon animation, monotone black and white.
```
