# Barker Blot Audio Notes

## Vocal Tone
- Pitch: Mid-range, slightly warbling and nervous (like a slightly deeper, more anxious early Mickey Mouse).
- Voice should sound like speaking into an old tin can—slightly muffled, vintage radio filter.
- Catchphrases: "Aw, ink spots!", "Well, that's just peachy...", "G-g-g-great tentacles!"

## Signature Sound Effects
- **Walking:** Soft, wet, "squish-squish-squish" instead of standard footfalls, like stepping in mud.
- **Startle Reaction:** A sudden, loud slide-whistle dropping in pitch, followed by wet splattering sounds.
- **Action/Gag Sounds:** When he pulls himself back together from a puddle, it sounds like thick pudding being rapidly slurped through a straw, followed by a bright, completely innocent "ding!".
