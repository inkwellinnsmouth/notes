**Name:** Barnaby "Barnacle" Bill
**The 1920s Archetype:** The Plucky Steamboat Sailor
**The Eldritch Twist:** His oversized anchor tattoo sometimes blinks with a real eye. His equivalent of a "spinach power-up" is a glowing green kelp that temporarily mutates his arm into an overpowering, thrashing tentacle.
**Visual Design:** Classic sailor suit, pie-cut eyes, tiny hat perched between his ears. Heavy charcoal sketch texture, monotone.
**Signature Animation Gag:** When startled, his sailor hat hovers, he blows out a steamboat puff from his ears, and one of his legs turns briefly into a writhing mass of squid tentacles before snapping back.
**Role in Innsmouth:** The oblivious, cheery delivery boy who brings cursed cargo to the dark, foggy town.
