# Barker Blot

## Backstory
Barker Blot has no memory of a childhood; he simply awoke one day on the inky, fog-drenched docks of Innsmouth. He assumes he's just a regular guy who happens to be a bit "drippy" sometimes. He works odd jobs around town to pay his rent at a dilapidated boarding house. He tries extremely hard to be cheerful and optimistic, whistle while he works, and maintain a 1920s "can-do" attitude, purely as a coping mechanism against the existential dread of his surroundings.

## Lore Connections
Barker is unknowingly a localized physical manifestation of a sleeping Old One's dream, drawn in the ink that stains the town. His very existence is an anomaly. The local cultists occasionally try to trap him to use his chaotic ink in rituals, but he usually escapes by accidentally slipping down the drain or breaking into a fast-paced Charleston dance until they get confused. 

The core rule of Barker: he can occasionally defeat minor antagonists using cartoon physics (e.g., pulling an anvil out of his overalls to drop on a deep one), but he is hopelessly outmatched by the true, incomprehensible cosmic horrors of the mythos.

## Relationships
- **The Mayor (Fishsticks Finn):** Barker constantly tries to get permits or help from the Mayor, completely oblivious to the Mayor's gills and predatory nature.
- **Penny "The Scoop" Parker (Concept):** She often takes pictures of Barker when he accidentally melts, trying to prove he's supernatural. Barker just thinks he's remarkably un-photogenic.
