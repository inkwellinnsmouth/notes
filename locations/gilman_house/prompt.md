# The "Gilman House" Hotel

## Archetype
The Grand (but Seedy) Hotel.

## The Eldritch Twist
The hotel is a literal labyrinth of shifting ink. The hallways stretch infinitely if you walk too fast, and the wallpaper patterns consist of "pie-cut" eyes. The elevators are cages made of "rib-bones" that groan as they ascend.

## Visual Design
A towering, rickety Victorian structure that sags to the left as if it’s trying to listen to the ocean. Film grain is heavy here, creating a "snowy" static effect in dark corners. The windows look like mismatched, blinking eyes, and the front door is a gaping, toothless "mouth" with a welcome mat made of dried seaweed.

## Signature Gag Elements
When a door closes, the doorknob turns into a literal "nose" that sneezes, blowing off hats and momentarily turning the room into a negative-exposure flash.
