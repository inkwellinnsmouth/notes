# Devil Reef Wharves

## Archetype
The Industrial Docklands.

## The Eldritch Twist
The water isn't liquid; it’s a thick, churning black ink wash that occasionally "reaches" up with liquid hands to grab at the pier. The "fog" here has a physical weight, appearing as swirling, charcoal-smudged clouds that take the shape of screaming faces.

## Visual Design
Rotting wooden piers that wiggle like spaghetti. The 16:9 frame sprocket holes appear "water-damaged" and "moldy." Devil Reef rises from the ink like a jagged, black tooth, with giant, rubbery shapes pulsating just beneath the surface.

## Signature Gag Elements
A 2D "Ghost Ship" frequently sails past, but it’s just a "cardboard cutout" that flips over every few seconds, revealing it has no "back side"—just more black ink.
