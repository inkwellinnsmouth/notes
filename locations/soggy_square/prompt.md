# The Soggy Square (Town Center)

## Archetype
The Bustling Main Street.

## The Eldritch Twist
The cobblestones aren't stone; they are smooth, calcified "scales" that ripple when stepped on. The town clock doesn't have numbers—it has thirteen shifting, weeping eyes, and the "Tick-Tock" sound is a wet, rhythmic heartbeat that slows down the closer you get to the water.

## Visual Design
Buildings like the "Marsh Refinery" lean inward at impossible, "rubber-hose" angles, nearly touching at the top to blot out the moon. Thick black ink "pools" in the gutters. Massive, silhouetted tentacles are woven into the wrought-iron streetlamps, which flicker with a dim, sickly luminescence.

## Signature Gag Elements
When the town clock "strikes" the hour, the entire street "squashes" flat like a stepped-on accordion, then "stretches" back up, causing all pedestrians to bounce three feet into the air.
